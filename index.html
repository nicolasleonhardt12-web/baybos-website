OUR STORY

Born American.
Raised on Asian flavor.

Baybos is what happens when the kid who grew up running around the family import warehouse gets to design the brand. Two kitchens, two languages, one obsession: food that would make both grandmas proud.

Our name comes from 百寶 — "a hundred treasures." That's what a real Asian pantry is: little packets, sauces and sacks of rice that turn any table into a feast.

No gatekeeping. No watered-down "fusion." Just the staples, done right, with packaging you can actually see through — literally.
